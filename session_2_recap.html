[01] DOWNTIME — BARRETT STATION

After the Node 7V incident, the crew spent approximately one week recovering at Barrett Station, Sector 379 on Verath Station. Injuries healed. The anonymous client received the full incident report — including the 25-year signal anomaly — and asked no follow-up questions. Payment cleared: a cargo unit of superconducting fiber.

  • Elias required professional medical care for his severe arm injury. Received treatment at Barrett Station.
  • Sector 379 is a standard ring sector — approximately five miles across, 8,115 miles long. Interior walls span about 12 miles. Primarily agricultural, with several major settlements. Vethara is the primary.
  • No new jobs. The crew sat around and spent money for a week.
[02] THE JOB — KETH AULRIC & SOREL VEV

The crew is approached in a cafe in Vethara's central square by an Undrel named Keth Aulric: six triangularly arranged eyes, long bioluminescent tendrils, cool grey skin. With her is Sorel Vev, a Callanthi delegate — small, muted in color, chromatophores cycling slow greens and golds that read as distress. Keth requests a private meeting.

"We have a concern. His name is Vethis. Vethis is a diplomat for Sector 379 and was a key vote for a council session in fourteen hours. They have suddenly disappeared. We fear the worst."
  • Vethis is an Ammonadda — a three-component knot registered as a single diplomatic entity. All three individuals share a psychic link. The knot's last known location is Perihelion, a local entertainment venue in the transit ring.
  • The vote is a sector council matter — equivalent to a state legislature. The crew will not need to fly anywhere; this is a city-level operation.
  • Keth's position: she does not know who is behind this. She suspects corporate or political opponents of the vote, but has no information to provide beyond a location.
  • Payment: Continuum favor + choice of class-two asset. Mission parameters transmitted directly to crew comms.
[03] PERIHELION — INFILTRATION
ARRIVAL
The crew arrives at Perihelion — an entertainment venue in the transit ring, advertised across all species. Full house. A Tharven bouncer — seven feet tall, all muscle — blocks the entrance; venue is at capacity. Elias charms their way past with influence.
INSIDE
Mara does a full assessment of the floor: standard camera arrangement covering all entry points, private rooms, and the main stage. The office door shows red — locked. A stairway leads up to VIP lounges. She earns a data point for Perihelion (plus one on future rolls here).

At the bar, K'thlx-kek and Elias order drinks — Elias buys a round for the party and the bartenders, working for information. The bartender says nothing useful. Yorra, a Callanthi dancer on stage, has one ear always angled toward the office door and is whispering to someone inaudibly. A clue for later.
OFFICE BREACH
Neyla uses her technologic ability to unlock the office door. K'thlx-kek psionically compels a bouncer to take an urgent bathroom break. Clifton lets a dozen people in off the street by unhooking the rope — floods the venue with chaos. Elias locks the compelled bouncer in the bathroom with physical lockpicks. Mara ducks into the office; the door closes behind her.
OFFICE RAID
Mara attempts to access the security terminal — completely locked out, countermeasures kick in. A bouncer comes through immediately. She shouts "I'm using the bathroom!" and then telekinetically shoves him back through the door. Takes a minor injury in the process. Clifton teleports himself and Mara outside — she's grabbed by a second bouncer mid-teleport. Her previously treated shoulder is re-dislocated. Major injury.
ESCAPE
Still inside: Elias is grabbed in a choke hold by another bouncer. He fires his laser eye from close range, severing the bouncer's arm below the elbow — cauterized. Takes a major non-lethal hit in retaliation. Clifton draws his weapon and commands the bouncer to stand down — partial success. The bouncer backs off. The crew evacuates through the front. Neyla, still inside, hacks the external security terminal and successfully downloads 48 hours of security footage. Escapes through a back route while Clifton's gun covers the door.
[04] ALLEY — FOOTAGE & DISCOVERY
SECURITY FOOTAGE
The crew reviews the downloaded footage in the alley outside Perihelion. The video shows all three Vethis components being escorted out of the club by individuals whose faces and body shapes have been digitally distorted by a countermeasure device. The footage cannot be cleaned up — the obfuscation is baked in at the camera level. One additional piece of data is recovered: a berth address — 7-Kova, Lot 14 — and a partial name: Feln.
THE RECYCLER
While reviewing footage, something is heard moving inside the large recycler unit beside them. The recycler hasn't been run yet. Neyla attempts to access the controls — takes a voltage shock (minor, rounds up to severe; she absorbs it) — and the door is thrown open. A feral Ammonadda tumbles out: no recognition in its eyes, moving like a rabid animal, no sapience visible. This is Vethis-Char, separated from the knot.
RESTRAINT
Mara attempts telekinesis to hold Vethis-Char — the creature breaks free before the field closes and attacks her. Major injury, rounds to severe. K'thlx-kek pins it with their staff; Elias deploys chloroform from his infiltration implant. The Ammonadda goes limp. Elias inhales the overflow and also passes out. Both are carried back to the ship.
IDENTIFICATION
Vethis-Char's garments are torn but an ID is found. Driver's license equivalent — identifies all three individuals: Vethis-Char, Vethis-Prenn, Vethis-Aun. Elias (while briefly conscious) makes an assessment roll and identifies the condition as Quorum Break — solo Ammonadda deteriorate rapidly without their knot, losing sapience in under 24 hours. A pair deteriorates more slowly but falls silent and mute.
[05] FOLLOW-UP INTEL
DIGITAL OBFUSCATION
Clifton and Mara research the countermeasure tech used to digitize the kidnappers' faces on camera. The tech is highly illegal, not traceable, and only available through high-end underworld markets. Asking around about it would almost certainly draw the wrong kind of attention. Dead end without the right contacts.
POND — DEAD END
Vethis-Char's wrist device still pings the other two components. Signal traces to a nearby public green space — specifically, the bottom of a small pond. Clifton dives in (sealed mesh suit) and finds a waterproof sack. Inside: clothing and personal computing devices belonging to Prenn and Aun. No bodies. Their belongings were stashed; they were moved.
TIME REMAINING
Treating injuries and recovering Elias from unconsciousness will take approximately eight hours. Of the original 14-hour window, roughly 5–6 hours remain when Session 3 begins.
