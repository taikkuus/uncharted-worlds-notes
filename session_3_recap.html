[01] SESSION OPEN — WHERE WE LEFT OFF
SESSION START
The crew is aboard the Lovelace, recovering from a disastrous run at Perihelion last session. Elias is just regaining consciousness. Eight hours of the original 14-hour window were burned on treatment and escape. ~5 hours remain before the Continuum corridor funding vote.
ABOARD SHIP
Vethis-Char — one component of the Ammonadda diplomatic knot — is in the Lovelace medbay in a solo-feral state. The other two components, Vethis-Prenn and Vethis-Aun, are unaccounted for but presumed held at Berth Row 7-Kova.
ADVANCEMENTS
Before departing, the crew tallies 4 advancement triggers from last session and spends experience on new skills. K'thlx-kek acquires Clairvoyance and immediately puts it to use.
[02] KETH AULRIC — FULL DISCLOSURE

Keth Aulric requests permission to board. Neyla meets her at the airlock and brings her to the conference room. Keth admits she has been withholding information and chooses to start fresh.

"I've not been completely forthcoming with you. I was protecting your three — but I thought it might complicate your decision to help. That was a mistake. It was also me not completely trusting you. I'm certain now."
  • Eleven days ago, the Continuum scheduled a vote on maintenance funding for corridor transit nodes — two nodes servicing significant bottom-out-of-frame movement, both overdue.
  • As an Ammonadda, Vethis is a registered triple-net diplomatic entity. Continuum quorum rules require physical presence to cast a vote. A separated knot cannot vote.
  • The opposition is the Tharven Sectoral Compact (TSC) — three sector reps with commercial interests in the Great Plains infrastructure corridor. The two projects compete for the same maintenance cycle budget.
  • This is not ideological. It is a budget competition. One side chose to remove the vote rather than win it.
  • Keth has tracked the political alignment for three months. She suspects TSC representatives but has no direct evidence yet. She will not name them without it.
  • She has identified a shell company: VOVREN FREIGHT, registered under a Tharven commercial proxy. Someone is layering themselves behind it.
[03] PSYCHIC RECONNAISSANCE — K'THLX-KEK

K'thlx-kek uses newly acquired Clairvoyance to attempt an assessment of Vethis-Prenn before the crew moves out.

Vision from Prenn's point of view: darkness, a small cramped space. Two heartbeats close together. Loud machinery outside — welders, forklifts. The container is not sealed for environment — faint light bleeds through a seam.
  • Confirms both Prenn and Aun are likely together in a sealed container in an industrial environment.
  • Machinery noise is consistent with a working berth or cargo dock.
  • A data point is earned — useful if the crew has to interact with whatever is in that container later.
[04] BERTH ROW 7-KOVA — INFILTRATION
TRANSIT
The crew takes public transit to Berth Row 7-Kova. The berth entrance requires key-card access. Neyla hacks it with a partial success — they get in, but whoever is inside is now aware of the breach.
INSIDE THE BERTH
A large working cargo bay: customs terminals, crates, a central forklift lane, four shielded doors. A Quorreni crane operator (Veth) watches from an enclosed operator station. A deep, croaking voice comes over the PA ordering them to leave. A Vovren Freight hauler is docked in the bay — the ship matches the registry from the shell company.
ESCALATION
K'thlx-kek uses Command to compel Veth to go on break, removing the crane operator from play. The unseen hostile — Ossin, a Gorrauth dock lieutenant — orders a squad of mechs deployed, including one heavy mech with a power fist and shoulder mount. Combat begins.
[05] COMBAT — BERTH 7-KOVA
ROUND 1
Kepler throws a concussive grenade — partial success. Light mechs are sensor-compromised and damaged but not destroyed. Neyla deploys her AI to invade the heavy mech, partially hijacking it. It turns and cuts the light mechs in half with its laser — then the laser fails to shut off, cutting a groove across the bay floor and destroying several crates (blue hydraulic fluid sprays out). Elias confirms the blue goo is not biological.
VACUUM EVENT
Ossin — in an EVA suit with magnetic boots on the upper level — deactivates the bay's external force field. Air begins evacuating. The crew grabs onto whatever they can. Mara loses her grip and is sucked toward open space, catching the top of a glass panel by her fingertips.
RESCUE
K'thlx-kek flies out into the evacuating air, catches Mara, and gets her back to the interior side of the force field generator — but something important is lost in the process. Mara loses her pinky finger, caught in the force field generator on the way through. Kepler finds an emergency panel and hits the big red button, restoring the force field and stopping the evacuation.
CLOSE COMBAT
Bulkhead doors open. Ossin descends and engages with his multi-tool, a large improvised wrench. Mara — bleeding, cradling her injured hand — tries to raise a barrier but is a half-second too slow, taking a major laceration on her head. K'thlx-kek's staff arcs electricity, electrifying Ossin's multi-tool and disarming him. Elias takes a shot on Ossin — partial success — dropping him but triggering a detonation countdown from the heavy mech's neck unit.
MECH DETONATION
Mara encases the mech in a barrier just in time. Bright flash. Muffled explosion. The barrier holds. Large scorch marks and smoke. The mech is gone.
[06] EXTRACTION — VETHIS FOUND
TERMINAL SEARCH
Elias accesses a terminal inside the bay. A search for "live cargo" locates a specific container — Package 32D. The box is opened with lockpicks and a crowbar. Inside: Vethis-Prenn and Vethis-Aun, both alive. No recognition in their eyes — calm but unresponsive. Not feral, but clearly deteriorating from 36+ hours as an incomplete knot.
OSSIN DETAINED
Ossin is identified by his ID badge — Ossin, Bunk Room 3. The crew restrains him with improvised zip-ties and has K'thlx-kek's assistants escort him back to the ship. He is stunned, sourly cooperative, and says nothing useful.
RETURN TO LOVELACE
Travel time: roughly one hour. The crew returns to the Lovelace medbay and reunites all three Vethis components with Vethis-Char, who is already aboard. Near-instant partial reintegration. The first coherent words out of the reconstituted knot: "Doveth Karrun."
[07] RESOLUTION — THE VOTE

Keth Aulric is contacted immediately. She boards with Sorel Vev and armed guards — Sorel's chromatophores scintillating with unrestrained joy. Vethis is escorted to the Continuum chambers with approximately three hours to spare.

The maintenance funding allocation passes by a single vote. The corridor transit nodes will receive their overdue maintenance. The Tharven Sectoral Compact's bid fails.

Vethis — speaking through Prenn — thanks the crew personally. They ask the crew to continue: find those responsible and help bring them to justice. The vote is won. The knot is intact. Doveth Karrun is named.
