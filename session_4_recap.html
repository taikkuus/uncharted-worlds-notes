[02] INTERROGATION — OSSIN

Ossin — the Gorrauth dock lieutenant captured at Berth 7-Kova — is restrained in the Lovelace medbay. K'thlx-kek leads the interrogation. Elias provides atmosphere by sourcing the largest syringe in the medbay and fidgeting with it in the background.

"I was just doing what I was told to do. I was told it shouldn't be found and that no harm should come to them. That's what I did. That's what I tried to do."
  • Person in charge: Feln Drevva. The name matches the partial from the Perihelion security footage — confirmed same individual.
  • Ossin was contracted to keep the crate from being discovered — not to harm the diplomats. He claims he has never killed anyone in his life. The crew is skeptical; he did attempt to depressurize the bay.
  • Ossin has a family — wife and children. He does not know the full chain above Feln. His information stops there.
  • Decision: keep Ossin confined aboard the Lovelace — useful as a potential witness and asset, not safe to release (he'd report back). Not killed. Eventually released with a strongly worded understanding.
[03] RESEARCH — FELN DREVVA & COLDWALL HOLDINGS
NETWORK SEARCH
Neyla accesses the station's public data network — partial success, not stealthy. Someone monitoring the data load would know someone is asking questions. Research confirms: Feln Drevva has ties to Coldwall Holdings, a security company headquartered in Sector 379. Vovren Freight exists mostly on paper — ships registered, no real officers or court filings findable. Coldwall Holdings is the operational avenue worth tracking.
ASSESSMENT
Elias uses his commercial career expertise to assess Coldwall Holdings as a security company. It appears to be a functioning operation — not purely a criminal front — but one with a flexible moral framework. Their offices are in a nearby warehouse district: a working facility with offices on the second level, security mechs patrolling the perimeter in three-unit groups, razor wire fencing.
[04] COLDWALL HOLDINGS — INFILTRATION
APPROACH
The crew takes a skycar to the warehouse district at night. Two patrol units of three mechs each. Razor wire fence perimeter. Elias uses bolt cutters from his infiltration kit — partial success. Mara times the mechs and keeps watch, elevating the cut to a full success. A section of fence is lifted and the whole crew gets through.
ENTRY
Neyla accesses the personnel door control panel — partial success. The door opens briefly. Clifton teleports himself and remaining crew members through the door before it locks again. Kepler (stealth) was already inside somehow, appearing mid-scene with a cigarette and half a bottle of booze. Nobody asks.
CONTACT
A voice on the PA orders them to leave. K'thlx-kek identifies the crew as following up on the diplomatic kidnapping. The lights cut. An Undrel steps into the light with hands raised — no weapons, no aggression. This is Feln Drevva. He confirms he ran the operation; says he is not authorized to name who gave him orders.
ESCALATION
Elsewhere in the warehouse, a human man is spotted at a far door. Kepler opens fire — headshot. The man drops. Immediately, return fire — Kepler takes a severe gunshot wound to the shoulder plus a bleeding debility. The voice changes on the PA. A new speaker announces himself and shuts down the mechs.
[05] PAHVEN RUSK — PRESIDENT, COLDWALL HOLDINGS

Elias makes a critical assessment roll and identifies the new speaker as Pahven Rusk — the president of Coldwall Holdings. His face is publicly known. Elias calls him by name. Rusk is cornered and chooses negotiation over escalation. His wounded companion is allowed to leave for medical attention. Mechs stand down.

"Pahven, this has gone wrong in so many ways. I think it's time that we just break up business... This has crossed too many of my moral objections."
— Feln Drevva, to Rusk
"I can confirm that I was working with Doveth Karrun. I can give you that information. That should not incriminate me."
— Pahven Rusk
  • Rusk provides an optical chip containing: full evidence linking Doveth Karrun (Tharven Sectoral Compact sector rep) to the operation, communications, arrangements, and payment chain.
  • The chip is conspicuously edited — Pahven Rusk's own name does not appear. He has scrubbed himself from the record.
  • Elias records the conversation on his survey kit data recorder. Rusk explicitly states on recording that he edited the data to remove his own involvement. This recording is retained as leverage.
  • Decision: the crew accepts the chip and the deal. No further violence. Rusk and Feln go their separate ways. The crew walks out.
  • Reward: favor with Coldwall Holdings (chosen over demanding Rusk's full exposure). The blackmail recording is kept as separate security.
[06] RESOLUTION — KNOT UNDONE CLOSED
DATA DELIVERED
The optical chip — evidence against Doveth Karrun, minus Rusk's self-incrimination — is transmitted to Keth Aulric and Sorel Vev. The mission is formally concluded. Doveth Karrun is implicated. What justice looks like from here is in the Continuum's hands.
OSSIN RELEASED
After the Coldwall situation resolves, Ossin is released with a strong suggestion to turn over a new leaf. He has never technically killed anyone and was hired for containment, not murder. He rubs his wrists and leaves, mildly grateful and clearly relieved. No further consequence assumed.
CRAMPED QUARTERS
Three days of recovery aboard the Lovelace. The GM calls for cramped quarters rolls. Everyone rolls well. Two results surfaced: Mara overheard Elias complaining about her performance to someone — creating friction. Clifton revealed his backstory to Kepler — orphaned as a young teen, enlisted at 18, identified as psionic at 19, recruited into the School's specialist program for a pay upgrade.
[07] NEW JOB — THE LONG CARRY

Three days into recovery, a message arrives. Recorded. A human woman, mid-thirties, professional, wearing a lab coat — seated at a terminal.

"My name is Doctor Liang Huxu. I'm hoping that you can help us with transport. There is someone waiting at Sector 97 — a Kaluarga called Oueikaru. They need transport to Thessavar. The pay is good. All that information you can find on the attached files."
  • Client: Dr. Liang Huxu, human xenobiologist. Already at destination (Thessavar) with the receiving party.
  • Passenger: Oueikaru — Kaluarga, third gender (matrix), semi-aquatic. Pick up at Sector 97.
  • Destination: Thessavar — the Pelhari homeworld.
  • Payment: described as good. Exact terms in attached files. Verification response expected in ~24 hours due to transmission delay.
  • Background check on Oueikaru: clean public record. Prolific matrix — has facilitated multiple interspecies births. Legally and culturally unremarkable by Kaluarga standards.
  • Kaluarga matrix role explained: three parties required for Kaluarga reproduction. The matrix provides genetic scaffolding to combine DNA. It is not a transactional or commercial arrangement; the motivations and methods are culturally distinct from human frameworks.
  • The crew will need to prepare for a semi-aquatic passenger — Oueikaru will require a suitable environment aboard the Lovelace. The medbay's smuggling compartments (Millennium Falcon-style, previously noted) can be reconfigured with removable plates to create a submersion pool.
