DOCUMENT CLASS: CREW RECORD — INTERNAL
REFERENCE: MXL-OPS-002

Session 2 — Sector 379, Verath Station

Delivery and Recovery

After approximately one week of recovery in Sector 379, the crew completed delivery of their findings from the Node 7V inspection to their contact. Payment was made in full: a cargo unit of superconducting fiber, not precious but not easily sourced. The contact left no forwarding information. She expressed no concern about the Crethul encounter. Whether that was confidence or compartmentalization was not apparent.

New Contract

While at a café in one of Sector 379's central squares, the crew was approached by Keth Aulric, an Undrel — a humanoid species with three pairs of eyes arranged in a triangular configuration and long kelp-like tendrils ending in luminous bulbs. She was accompanied by Sorel Vev, a Lumifloof whose chromatophores cycled through muted greens and golds, reading as anxious and distressed.

Keth offered a job and declined to discuss it in public. The crew settled on a nearby alley.

Sorel Vev's communiqué, relayed through Keth: a colleague — Vethis, an Ammonadda composite individual comprising three components (Vethis-Char, Vethis-Prenn, and Vethis-Aun) — was a key vote in an upcoming Sector 379 legislative session. Vethis had disappeared. The vote was fourteen hours away. Sorel described Vethis as more outgoing than most Ammonadda, and noted the last known location was somewhere on the station's local substrate training lane rights circuit — imprecise.

The reward offered: a favor with the Continuum, plus a class-2 asset of the crew's choosing.

Perihelion

The crew traced Vethis to Perihelion, a venue in Sector 379. Getting inside required working the door — influence, a well-timed distraction, and patience. Once in, the crew spread across the floor, gathering what they could while Neyla identified a locked back office with a security terminal inside.

Neyla hacked the office door. Mara went in. She located the security terminal and attempted to pull footage but was caught in the process — the system locked her out and a bouncer moved on her. Clifton teleported Mara out before the situation escalated further. Neyla, now with line of sight to the locked terminal from outside the door, accessed the system remotely and downloaded the footage.

The footage showed all three components of Vethis being led out of Perihelion together, accompanied by individuals whose faces and body shapes had been digitally distorted by an active countermeasure. The technology involved is highly illegal, available only through deep underworld connections, essentially untraceable, and not something that can be investigated without drawing significant attention from the wrong parties.

One clean lead was recovered from the footage: address 7-Kova, Lot 14, and the name Feln.

The Recycler

While reviewing the footage in the alley behind Perihelion, the crew heard movement inside one of the large recyclers. They forced it open. One of Vethis's three components — feral, non-verbal, eyes showing no sapience — emerged and attacked.

The crew established what they could: Ammonadda do not function well in isolation. Within roughly 24 hours of separation from their other components, individuals become feral. Vethis had been taken the night before. The timeline fit.

Subduing the component was not clean. Mara attempted to hold it with telekinesis and took major injuries when it broke through and hit her. Representative K'thlx-kek pinned it with his staff. Elias administered a chemical sedative from his infiltration kit — and inhaled enough of it himself to lose consciousness.

The component was secured and identified. Personal effects confirmed: Vethis-Char, Vethis-Prenn, and Vethis-Aun. One of the three was now in custody.

The Pond

A device scan located two additional signals approximately 50 meters away, at the bottom of a small pond in a nearby green space. Clifton dove and recovered a submerged sack containing clothing and personal computer devices. No Vethis components were present at the location. They had been there. They were not anymore.

State at Session End

  • Elias: unconscious, recovering from sedative exposure.

  • Multiple crew members carrying injuries of varying severity.

  • Approximately five to eight hours remaining before the Sector 379 legislative vote.

  • One Vethis component in custody aboard the Lovelace — sedated, condition unstable.

  • Two Vethis components unaccounted for. Last known trace: the pond. Now gone.

  • Next lead: 7-Kova, Lot 14. Name on record: Feln.

  • The countermeasure technology used on the kidnappers — illegal, high-market, underworld-sourced. Investigating it will draw attention.
